How to Play:

Dominoes may be placed anywhere on the board. They may be taken from the stockpile and placed at the side until you think you have found the correct position. The white answer areas will turn green if the correct line score of 21 is reached. They will turn red if the line scores exceed 21.

The Easy Start button will place 6 dominoes in their correct places, allowing you to have a helping hand. The Solution button will only become active if certain lines are filled in and their totals equal 21. These are highlighted if the button is clicked. See if you can complete it.
